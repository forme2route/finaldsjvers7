PRACTICE TESTS
Test Questions & Answers made easier with practice The first step to take before taking the written test and doing the practice tests is to get an updated copy of The Art of Driving drivers handbook. This book will have the information you will need to know about the driving laws rules and precautions in Jamaica. Read and study the material in this book as it will be your source material for the written test. The test is divided into two sections to test your knowledge of the Traffic signals and lights and knowledge of the rules of the road. There are more than 100 rules of the road in Jamaica and it is necessary to study all the traffic signs and lights and the rules. The questions are randomly chosen before you take the test. It is better to study everything These online practice tests, with multiple choice questions and answers will prepare you for the Road Code Test, the Provisional Drivers Licence (Learners Licence) and the Jamaican Drivers Licences tests that are required before you can get your Drivers Licence. N.B. These practice test are for everyone and will also inform drivers of the new and existing rules and laws of the road, refresher courses and defensive driving. To successfully prepare, consider using these practice test and study the Art of Driving handbook as a guide. The practice tests are free and will allow you to practice and prepare as much as possible prior to taking the real tests. Take these practice tests repeatedly. N.B This is not the actual test. These are the most probable and frequent questions and answers. They are factual and you will need to engage them whenever you use the roads. The tests have 24 or 34 or 40 questions and you will need to get 20 or 30 or 36 right to pass.
